Disconnect Switches

BREMAS AC disconnectors are available from 16A up to 1600A and are built to ensure the maximum reliability.

  • DS Series - Rated thermal current for open switch from 25A to 100A, 690 to 800V rated insulation voltage, IP20 terminal protection degree, without fuse holder
  • TS, TSC/TSD Series - Rated thermal current for open switch from 125A to 1600A, 1000V rated insulation voltage, without fuse holder
  • XNF Series - Rated current from 16A to 32A, 600V rated insulation voltage, with fuse holder
  • TF Series - Rated current from 63A to 630A, IP55 handle protection degress, base mounting


The double brak silver alloy contacts have positive opening to quarantee high electrical efficiency and safety. Handles have up to IP55 protection degree Enclosed series with IP65 protection degree are available on request.
