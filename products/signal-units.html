Push Buttons and Signal Devices

Control and Signalling series are composed by pushbuttons and selector switches designed for quick and easy installation. They are available with thermoplastic fixing ring or with metal screw fixing, suited to ensure a more secure and stable positioning of the unit on the panel.

  • BP Series: fixing hole ø22 mm, illuminated or not, push buttons, selectors, key selectors, emergency mushroom
  • BM Series: fixing hole ø22 mm, push buttons, mushroom push buttons, selector, key selector, LED inducators
  • Signal Towers - BT Series
  • Push Button Stations: singal push button, double push button, triple push button stations, emergency mushroom
  • Foot Switches - PS, PC Series: with or without protection, thermoplastic or metal foot switches


ERSCE Control and signalign units are designed to ensure accuracy, reliability, high mechanical and electrical life. All buttons are certified IP65, (IP66 with optional dustproof cover), while foot switches are IP54. The NC contacts are manufacted with positive opening for safety function. The terminal screws with self raising plate are supplied in open position for an easier and quicker wiring operation. Control and signalling series can be equipped with a wide range of accessories.
