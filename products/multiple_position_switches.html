Multiple Position Switches

The FMV series vertical mounting multiple position switches, are an important accessory in the automatic production process for monitoring the moving parts of a machine. Due to their limited dimensions in proportion to the functions performed, the multiple position switches offer the ideal solution to command automatic machines. the body of multiple positon switches is made of die cast metal alloy, while the roller plunger actuators are made of tempered steel.


  • Mounting brackets - 100: 130 for the start of the first actuators at 30 mm
  • Mounting brackets - 200: 230 for the start of the first actuators at 30 mm
  • Distances between the roller plungers: 12mm or 16 mm
