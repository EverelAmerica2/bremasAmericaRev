Limit and Safety Switches

The position switches are ERSCE’s main product, and are the result of more than 40 years of experience in industrial automation.


They are suitable for use even in the toughest environmental conditions, thanks to their solid construction, IP66 certified, and proven reliability over decades of fieldwork.


ERSCE position switches are available in 4 different series E100, E200, E300, E400, each characterized by different technical solutions and mounting options with standard thread for cable input/output pg 13.5. On request, the American Standard is available with thread for cable ½” NPT and M20


Each series comprises a metal actuator, the casing is available in PA6 thermoplastic reinforced with fiberglass or diecast metal alloy. Maximum versatility is guaranteed by the adjustable actuators in steps of 90°, by the lever adjustment every 10° for 360° and by the lever activation to the left or right only or in both directions.


  • E100 Series - 1 cable entry, most compact Bremas ERSCE limit switches
  • E200 Series - 2 cable entries, designed for max. installation flexibility
  • E300 Series - 3 cable entries, designed to withstand to the most severe conditions
  • E400 Series - 1 cable entry, most robust Bremas ERSCE limit switches
  • E600, E700 Series - pre-wired limit switches with 1 cable entry
