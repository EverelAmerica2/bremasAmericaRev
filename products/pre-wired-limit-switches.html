Pre-Wired Limit Switches

The E600, E700 series pre-wired limit switches offer the same characteristics of reliability, accuracy and robustness of the other Bremas ERSCE switches in compact size.


Thanks to their redusced dimensions, the precision of their actuators, the practical mounting system and the high protection against the introduction of liquids and powders (IP67), these devices are suitable for use on machines where space is restricted and working environment is tough.


E600 and E600 series are quipped with 4 terminals double-break contact blocks, with galvanically seperated moving contacts.


The drived are by snap action, 1NO + 1NC with positive opening of the NC contact


  • E600: cable on the bottom, metal body and actuators
  • E700: cable on the side, plastic or metal body, metal actuators
