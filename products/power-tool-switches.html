Power Tool Switches

Bremas ERSCE power tool switches are suitable for power and electromechanical tools and are available with lever and trigger options.

  • LAA65, LAA88 Series: 2 pole, trigger type switch, with or without lock
  • LEA202R Series: 2 pole, stable lever
  • LLA42 series: 2 pole with stable or unstable lever
  • LFA203 series: 3 pole, stable lever
  • LLA43 Series: 3 pole with stable or unstable lever
  • LNA45 Series: 3 pole, stable lever
