Cam Switches

Bremas offers 4 series of cam switches:

  • CR series from 12 to 40A, 690V rated insulation voltage, IP20 terminals protection degree, IP65 handles protection degree
  • CA series from 12 to 630A, 690V rated insulation voltage, IP00 terminals protection degree, up to IP65 handles protection degree
  • CQ series from 12 to 32A, 690V rated insulation voltage, IP20 terminals protection degree, IP65 handles protection degree, easy made wiring
  • Enclosed series with front or lateral drive, up to IP65 protection degree


And a wide range of accessories.


All the most widespread fixing options are available: rear mounting with screws, rear mounting with central hole ø 22mm (0.87”) with locking nut, base mounting on DIN rail or with screws, escutcheon plate for standard 45mm knock-out.


Various schemes are available: ON-OFF switches, change-over switches, motor control switches, voltmeter and ammeter switches, multi step switches. A large number of special schemes are available on request.
