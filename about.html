Bremas America

Bremas America is a Georgia USA Corporation dedicated to serving the American Market. Bremas America is focused on Marketing, Promoting and supplying the Bremas and Ercse brand product lines. Being responsive to customers in quality, delivery, reliability and professionalism is a primary goal for our company.

Our headquarters is located in Atlanta Georgia. The facilities in Georgia are also where our distribution and customer service center operates.

The merging of two well established brand names, Bremas and Ercse has created strong global brand recognition synonymous with quality and reliability. These two companies are major players in the design, manufacturing, and marketing of electromechanical components. With over 40 years of experience, Bremas Ercse is the industrial sector leader for both standard and customized control products.

Bremas Ercse is committed to the belief that its long term success depends on its ability to fully satisfy customers’ requests. All the companies’ functions are in accordance with UNI EN ISO9001:2008. All technical data for the products are verified, manufactured and inspected in accordance with the most stringent standards demanded by the current legislation to guarantee performance, durability and safety. Bremas Ercse has a state of the art research and development facility committed to new products design and the updating of existing products to meet the needs of the customer. A wide range of experience has been developed in design and manufacturing of custom products for the industrial sector by our technicians. Our technicians work and constantly interact with the customers, to develop a solution for every field of applications and also the most specific requests.

Bremas Ercse CAM Switches and Disconnector Switches are able to satisfy many different field applications. These products are used in a wide range of industrial applications, including: railroad, food processors, welding machinery, and elevators, assemble lines, power tools, pumps, HVAC systems, heating elements, transformers, and circuit breakers.
